### Changes Made (Revised Approach):
1. Replaced `implementation(libs.compose.desktop)` with `implementation(compose.desktop.currentOs)` in app/build.gradle.kts
2. Removed platform-specific Linux-only `runtimeOnly(libs.skiko.linux.x64)` dependency
3. Removed `skiko-linux-x64` entry from gradle/libs.versions.toml (no longer needed with compose.desktop.currentOs)

### Rationale:
The Compose Multiplatform library provides `compose.desktop.currentOs` which automatically resolves to the correct platform-specific Skiko runtime at build time:
- Linux: Resolves to skiko-awt-runtime-linux-x64
- macOS: Resolves to skiko-awt-runtime-macos-x64 (or arm64)
- Windows: Resolves to skiko-awt-runtime-windows-x64

This makes the build truly cross-platform without hardcoding platform-specific references.
